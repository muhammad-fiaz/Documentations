## Get Involved with the Community

Welcome to our community! We're excited to have you join us and contribute to the growing ecosystem of applications and tools we’re building together.

### Contributing

There are many ways to contribute and help our community thrive:

- **Documentation:** Improve our documentation by suggesting edits or adding new sections to make it more accessible and user-friendly.
- **Codebase:** Fix bugs, propose new features, or refine existing functionalities in our repositories. Explore our work on [GitHub](https://github.com/muhammad-fiaz);
- **Open Source Projects:** Collaborate on our open-source initiatives. Check out our [repositories](https://github.com/fiaztechnologies) and see how you can get involved.

### Discussions

Connect with other members, ask questions, and share your expertise:

- **[GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/orgs/fiaztechnologies/discussions)** – Join ongoing discussions, ask technical questions, and share insights.
- **[Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/Kpm7frtu)** – Chat with community members in real time.

### Social Media

Stay in the loop by following us on social media:

- **GitHub:** Follow us on [GitHub](https://github.com/muhammad-fiaz) for the latest updates.

### Code of Conduct

We strive to create an inclusive and respectful environment. Please follow our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/muhammad-fiaz/devfolio/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to ensure a positive experience for everyone.

Familiarize yourself with the Code of Conduct to help maintain a welcoming community for all members.

---

If you have any questions or need support, feel free to reach out via our community discussions. Your engagement and contributions make this community what it is. Thank you for being a part of it!
